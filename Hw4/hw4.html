The Big 3


  • Nintendo
  • Playstation
  • Xbox

Nintendo

Started out as a playing card business in the 1800s. Its focus shifted to toys and electronics in the 1900s. Created the Game Watch in 1980 and would release the NES in 1985. Nintendo has become the most popular gaming company and has had several successful games such as Super Mario, Zelda, and Metroid along with several consoles.


Playstation

Spawned from a broken collaboration between Sony Interactive Entertainment and Nintendo in 1994. The company would go on to create 5 consoles and 2 handheld gaming devices that became popular worldwide. Responsible for popular game franchises like God of War, Uncharted, and Gran Turismo.


Xbox

Subdivision of Microsoft created in the early 2000s to compete with Nintendo and Sony Playstation. Known for taking risks and innovating in the console sector even to its own detriment. Created games such as Halo, Gears of War, and Fable. There are currently 3 generations of Xbox consoles.
